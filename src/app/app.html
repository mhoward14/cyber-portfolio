I'm a retired Air Force veteran with 21 years of service, specializing in governance, risk, and compliance. My career began in network, system, and client administration — working across IAM, Active Directory, and endpoint management — before evolving through information management and ultimately into physical and information security roles.

I hold active CompTIA Security+, CySA+, and PenTest+ certifications alongside the (ISC)² CC credential, with SecurityX and ISACA CISM currently in progress. I'm also completing my M.S. in Cybersecurity & Information Assurance at Western Governors University (May 2026). My TS/SCI clearance is eligible for reinstatement.

My deepest expertise is in the governance and compliance side of the NIST RMF lifecycle — security control assessment, ATO package development, POA&M management, and SSP development — supporting environments across NATO, NORAD/USNORTHCOM, and the U.S. Air Force.
